Welcome to Villa Aelia

Experience the perfect blend of luxury, tranquility, and heartfelt hospitality. Our villa is your gateway to the breathtaking beauty and vibrant culture of Corfu.

For over three decades, our family has been dedicated to welcoming guests from around the globe. Villa Aelia was born out of our passion for creating unforgettable experiences, where luxury meets the charm of Corfu.
